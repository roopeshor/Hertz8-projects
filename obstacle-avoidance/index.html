Autonomous Navigation

Obstacle Avoidance Rover

Overview

An autonomous mobile robot capable of navigating unknown environments by detecting and evading physical obstructions. The system uses ultrasonic echolocation to "see" its surroundings and make real-time decisions to stop, reverse, or turn.

Technical Stack

  • Arduino Uno: Central processing unit.
  • Ultrasonic Sensor: Measures distance via echo timing.
  • Servo Motor: Sweeps the sensor to extend the field of view.
  • DC Gear Motors & L298N Driver: Manage motion control.

Impact

This architecture serves as a critical educational prototype for self-driving vehicles, warehouse automation, and service robotics, solving the problem of collision-free movement without human oversight.
